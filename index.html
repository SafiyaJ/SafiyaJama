Projects

Orange Omsorg

Type: Website

For: Client

Role: Freelancer

I was the project manager and developer for an online virtual hackathon. We solved the communication problem between healthcare workers and relatives of patients in the Covid-19 crisis and I managed the project in an agile team. I, along with my team, conducted user research, designed mockups with accessibility in mind and used React Native to create a prototype. Within a month, we ideated and programmed an app as proof of concept and presented it to a panel of judges.

Our submission awarded us the position of the Most Innovative project in the entire competition.
